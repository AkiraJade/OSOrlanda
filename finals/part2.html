PART 2: ADDING USERS

Domain User Management for TUP-Taguig Departments

This video demonstrates the comprehensive creation and management of domain users across all TUP-Taguig departments. The process includes creating Organizational Units (OUs) for each academic department and administrative office, then distributing IT student names to their respective sections. Key configurations include:

Academic Departments:
• Basic Art and Sciences (BTVTE Electrical, Electronics, Computer Hardware, Computer Software)
• Electrical and Allied (Information Technology, EE, ECE, ICT, Mechatronics)
• Mechanical and Allied (MET, RAC, NDT, EMET, Automotive, ElectroMechanical)
• Civil and Allied (Civil Engineering, CET, Environmental Science, Chemical Engineering Technology)

Administrative Offices:
• Registrar's Office, Director's Office, Research & Extension, HR Management
• Finance Office, Supply & Procurement, Records Office, Library Services

All users are configured with specific logon restrictions, password policies (30-day expiration), and workstation assignments based on their departmental roles.
